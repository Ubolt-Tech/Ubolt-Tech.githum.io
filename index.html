About Business

The Driving Trucking Modernization

Road logistics plays a major and critical role in overall logistics environment and covers 40-60% of the total material shipped throughout the year. Despite its big size this industry is marred with high operational inefficiencies, poor working conditions and still considered as an unorganised sector of economy. Due to this it has adverse cascading effects on GDP & WPI.

Team @ Ubolt Technologies with their deep experience of the sector has understood that technology alone cannot solve the age old issue with trucking industry. With process analysis, close group interviews and learning industry best practices around the world we had redesigned the business excellence system and weaves the relevant technology around it.
